SUBJECT: A ninja — the stealthy scout-assassin of the steel-blue army. A human in a dark, close-fitting ninja outfit: a face-covering mask and hood so only the eyes show, dark charcoal-and-steel-blue garb (the scouts' dark shade of the army palette), light and unarmored for speed. Keep the SAME chibi head proportion as the rest of the roster (about 2.5 heads tall) — the mask and hood already bulk up the head, so do NOT make an oversized super-deformed giant head on a tiny body; keep a balanced chibi build. He holds a single kunai (a short black throwing dagger with a ring pommel) in one hand, ready in front of the body; the other hand is empty (an open or lightly clenched hand) near a belt pouch — just ONE kunai, NO shuriken, NO throwing stars, NOT swords or ninjato. Silent, sharp, focused eyes. Distinctly a masked shinobi versus the hooded thief. Keep the mature, muted, limited palette.
