SUBJECT: A human cleric — the basic healer and objective-capturer of the steel-blue army. Same steel-blue faction palette and same face style as the fighter, but a gentle support priest, not a soldier. A simple hooded robe in steel-blue and off-white cloth with small gold trim. In both hands, held up in front of the chest, he clasps a holy cross — a chunky, clearly readable hand cross / crucifix of wood and gold, the main role prop. NO staff and NO wand (a staff reads as a mage); the cross is what marks him as a cleric. Calm, kindly, devout expression. The plainest of the holy orders — no armor, no ornate mitre. Keep the mature, muted, limited palette.
