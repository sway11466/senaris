SUBJECT: A kunoichi — a female ninja of the steel-blue army, a nimble scout who fights with ninjutsu (magic). The same dark scout palette as the male ninja: close-fitting dark charcoal-and-steel-blue ninja garb, light and unarmored for speed. A woman, slender and agile, with a lowered face scarf or partial mask (her face more visible than the fully-masked male ninja) and a dark ninja hood or tied-back hair. She carries NO blades — her only prop is a ninjutsu scroll: a rolled paper makimono held up in her hands in front of her body as she casts, with a faint magical glow around it, clearly marking her as a magic-casting shinobi. Sharp, cool, focused expression. Distinctly female and magic-based versus the masked, dual-blade male ninja. Keep the mature, muted, limited palette.
