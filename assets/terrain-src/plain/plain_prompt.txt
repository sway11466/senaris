SUBJECT: A patch of plain grassland — calm, short grass on PREDOMINANTLY UNIFORM ground. The base ground color is a light, soft muted sage green, hex #B4C6A0 — use this exact base green and keep it consistent across all variants. Keep the color even and low-contrast: at most very subtle tonal shading, and NO large bare-earth or brown dirt patches (large patches read as wasteland and repeat badly when tiled). Only a FEW small grass tufts and tiny pebbles, scattered randomly and unevenly with bare gaps. Sparse, quiet, mostly plain sage-green. No paths, no rows, no grid.
