SUBJECT: A goblin grunt — the basic rank-and-file raider of a goblin horde. Sickly olive-green skin, a small hunched wiry body, long pointed ears, a wide impish grin with a snaggly tooth or two, and big sly yellow eyes. Crude scavenged gear: patchwork brown scrap leather, rough cloth rags and a strap of rusty scrap iron — everything shoddy and looted, nothing polished or military. He grips a crude chipped cleaver-like short blade upright in front with both hands. Keep the same mature, muted limited palette as the rest of the game's units — dull olive greens and browns, NOT bright cartoon green.
